Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CleanDunk, developed by Jan Kosi, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SloVenture Android application.

By using SloVenture, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use SloVenture:

  • Account information — your username and email address when you register or sign in with Google
  • Location data — your GPS location to detect nearby points of interest and mark visited locations. Background location is optional and only used if you enable it
  • Photos — photos you take within the app at points of interest, uploaded to Firebase Storage
  • Visit data — which POIs you have visited, marked, or photographed
  • Device information — basic device data collected automatically by Firebase (crash reports, analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalise the SloVenture experience
  • To save your visited POIs, photos, and progress across devices
  • To send proximity notifications when you are near a point of interest (only if you enable notifications)
  • To track your journey and display stats in your profile
  • To improve the app and fix bugs using anonymised crash data

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), hosted in the European Union. We use Firebase Authentication to protect your account.

We implement industry-standard security measures. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Location Data

SloVenture uses your device location to detect nearby points of interest and automatically mark visits. Location is only collected while using the app, unless you explicitly enable background location tracking.

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings. Without location, proximity detection and automatic visit marking will not work, but you can still manually mark POIs as visited.

5. Photos

Photos you take within SloVenture are uploaded to Firebase Storage and linked to your account. These photos are private and only visible to you. You can delete your photos at any time within the app.

6. Third-Party Services

SloVenture uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, storage (Privacy Policy)
  • MapLibre / MapTiler — map rendering (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Play Services — sign-in and location (Privacy Policy)
  • Wikimedia Commons — POI images under Creative Commons licences

7. Children's Privacy

SloVenture is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent for background location or notifications at any time
  • Export your data upon request

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@cleandunk.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data including visit history, photos, and profile information will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of SloVenture after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
